ADCA - Advance Diploma in Computer Application

What is ADCA certificate?

ADCA, an acronym for Diploma in Computer Application, is a vocational certification program that aims to upgrade an individual's computer skills, enhancing their employability.

What is the qualification for ADCA?

Candidates must have passed graduation or equivalent level (with Mathematics as a subject of study at 10+2 or graduation level) examination from a recognized university. He/she must have obtained at least 50% (relaxable for reserved category candidates) of the marks at graduation or equivalent level.

Which subject is ADCA?

Diploma in Computer Applications is a certificate course to study computer applications in depth. The course imparts theoretical, practical and technical knowledge to its learners about various computer tools that are used in day to day life.
