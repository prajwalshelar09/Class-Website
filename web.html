What is a Web Development ? Web development refers to the creating, building, and maintaining of websites. It includes aspects such as web design, web publishing, web programming, and database management. It is the creation of an application that works over the internet

What is the qualification for web development ?

Candidates must have passed graduation or equivalent level (with Mathematics as a subject of study at 10+2 or graduation level) examination from a recognized university. He/she must have obtained at least 50% (relaxable for reserved category candidates) of the marks at graduation or equivalent level.
